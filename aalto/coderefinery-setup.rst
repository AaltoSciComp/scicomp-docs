CodeRefinery
============

These days, just refer to the main installation instructions.  See
your particular course's webpage for the exact needs for your course.
The general instructions are here:
https://coderefinery.github.io/installation/

Some miscellaneous notes for Aalto managed laptops:

* Installing Anaconda from the Aalto software center doesn't let you
  make your own environments.  We recommend installing Miniforge
  yourself, in your own user account (basically the instructions say).
