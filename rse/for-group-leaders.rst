RSE info for group leaders
==========================

Aalto Research Software Engineers are part of Science-IT, a research
infrastructure in the School of Science.  We serve everyone in Aalto,
though.

If your group needs computing, data, or research software development,
but it's something that you don't have time to do or it isn't your
specialty, we can help with that while you focus on your actual
science.  We are all former researchers ourselves and can seamlessly
work with you.



Small support: "Garage" office hour
-----------------------------------

If you or your group has any short term problems and want to talk to a
specialist in computing, send them to the :doc:`Scientific Computing
Garage </help/garage>` any workday at 13:00.  In it, you can ask
anything and usually get an answer, or at least a pointer to the
answer.  You can, of course, also make use of this yourself to contact
us to discuss your plans.

The garage is the starting point for small projects which go beyond
garage but still are mostly in your hands.

**Tell your group member's it's OK to ask for help.**



Larger projects
---------------

We also do larger projects, for example developing a significant piece
of software or service for your project.  See :doc:`portfolio` for
examples.  For these, we often act as part of your research team for
the duration.

Depending on your unit, you get various amounts of time for free: the
idea is that it's better to help with medium size things than get
stuck up in finance practices.  For supporting units (currently
mostly SCI), we can do amount a month of work "for free".  Beyond
that, we'll discuss and use some other funding.

If you have an idea, either current or future, we recommend discussing
with us as soon as possible.


Preparing ideas
---------------

If you have a future idea or are writing a grant, we can help with
planning your ambitions to see what is even possible.  Writing in our
services can make your grant more competitive, see :doc:`/about/for-proposals`.
